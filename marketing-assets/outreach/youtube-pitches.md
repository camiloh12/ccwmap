# YouTube creator outreach

**Rules:**
- Pitch via the channel's "About" tab business email
- Don't ask for a full video review. Ask for "30 seconds in a tools-I-use segment"
- Skip channels >500k subs unless you have a personal connection — the bigger the channel, the lower the response rate

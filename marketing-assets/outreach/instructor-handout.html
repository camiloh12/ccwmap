CCW Map

Free, crowd-sourced map of where you can and can't legally carry. Built by a CCW permit holder.

No ads No tracking Works offline

What it does

  • Map of gun-free zones across all 50 states
  • Color-coded: green / yellow / red
  • Restriction tags: federal, schools, posted businesses, airport, government
  • Tap a POI to mark it
  • Works offline once cached

What you should know

  • No account needed to view the map
  • Email/password account only to add or correct pins
  • Always confirm posted signage on site — the map is a starting point, not legal advice
  • Not affiliated with any government agency or political organization

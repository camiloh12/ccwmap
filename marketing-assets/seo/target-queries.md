## Page-priority order (write in this order)

1. **#1 gun free zones near me** — anchor page, links to all others
2. **#6 30.06 vs 30.07** — Texas, highest individual-query volume in this set
3. **#13 NY CCIA sensitive places** — NY carriers are starved for guides
4. **#10 California GFSZA** — high search, low competition
5. **#15 Florida permitless** — recent law change drives traffic
6. **#18 concealed carry post office** — short page, easy win
7. Continue down the list

## What to skip

- Anything that requires legal interpretation of an active court case
- "Is X stand-your-ground state" — covered well elsewhere, not your wedge
- Generic "concealed carry tips" — too competitive
- Anything that triggers Google's "your money or your life" (YMYL) rules without proper legal review

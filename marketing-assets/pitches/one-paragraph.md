# One-paragraph pitch (≤500 chars)

## Primary

> CCW Map is a free, crowd-sourced map of where concealed-carry permit holders can and can't legally carry across the United States. Permit holders mark gun-free zones — federal property, schools, posted private businesses, secure airport areas — and everyone benefits. The app works offline, runs no ads, requires no account to view the map, and stores no personal location history. Built and maintained by a CCW holder, for CCW holders.

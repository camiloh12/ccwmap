## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
CCW Map is a free, crowd-sourced map of gun-free zones across the United States.
Concealed-carry permit holders mark restricted places — federal property, schools,
posted businesses, secure airport areas — and everyone benefits.

No ads. No tracking. No account required to view the map.

▸ KEY FEATURES

• Crowd-sourced gun-free-zone map covering all 50 states
• Three-color carry status: green (allowed), yellow (uncertain), red (no carry)
• Detailed restriction tags: federal property, state/local government, K-12 schools,
  airport secure areas, posted private businesses, and more
• Works offline — caches the map and your nearby pins
• Tap any point of interest to mark it; tap an existing pin to see details
• US-boundary validated to prevent stray pins
• Optional account for adding and editing pins (view-only requires no account)

▸ WHO IT'S FOR

• CCW / concealed-carry permit holders looking up sensitive places before traveling
• New permit holders learning the rules in their state
• Carriers in states with complex sensitive-place laws (CA, NY, NJ, IL, MA)
• Texas LTC holders tracking 30.06 / 30.07 / 51% signs
• Anyone who wants to know whether a business they're about to enter is posted

▸ HOW IT WORKS

1. Open the app, no account needed.
2. Browse the map. Pins are color-coded by carry status.
3. Tap a pin to see why it's marked (which restriction applies, whether it's posted, etc.).
4. Sign in to add or correct a pin. Any authenticated permit holder can update any pin —
   crowd-sourced moderation keeps the data accurate.

▸ PRIVACY

• No analytics SDKs. No ad pixels. No third-party trackers.
• No location history collection. The app uses your location to center the map; that's it.
• No selling user data. There is no user data to sell.
• Account login (email/password via Supabase Auth) is only required to add or edit pins.

▸ STATE-LAW COVERAGE

CCW Map handles the major restriction categories under federal and state law:
• Gun-free school zones (GFSZA, 18 USC 922(q))
• Federal facilities (18 USC 930)
• Texas: 30.06 (concealed), 30.07 (open), 51% (alcohol), 46.03 (government property)
• California: PC 626.9 posted-property, SB 2 sensitive places
• New York: post-Bruen CCIA sensitive places
• Illinois: 430 ILCS 66/65 prohibited places
• Florida: 790.06(12) prohibited places under HB 543
• Plus: airport secure areas, state/local government, K-12, federal courthouses

▸ WHAT THIS APP IS NOT

• Not legal advice. Always confirm posted signage on site.
• Not a replacement for state-specific legal training.
• Not affiliated with any government agency, gun-rights organization, or political party.

▸ FREE TODAY

CCW Map is free, with no ads, no premium tier, and no in-app purchases.
There are no current plans to monetize. If hosting ever outgrows what one
developer can absorb, donations and grants come first. Selling user data
is not on the table.

Built by a CCW permit holder. Open to feedback at camilo@kyberneticlabs.com.
```

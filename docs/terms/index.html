6. Reporting and Blocking

To keep the map useful and safe, the app provides two in-app tools that work without filing a support ticket:

  • Report a pin. Tap any pin and choose Report. Pick a reason and add an optional note. Reports are queued for moderator review.
  • Block a user. From a pin's detail dialog you can block its creator. Pins authored by users you have blocked are hidden from your map immediately and stay hidden on your device.

Moderation response time. We commit to reviewing reports of objectionable content or abusive behavior and taking appropriate action within 24 hours of receipt. Action may include removing the offending content, suspending the responsible account, or both.

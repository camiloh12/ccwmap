Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how to request deletion of your CCW Map account and associated data.

⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible.

Once your account is deleted, you will NOT be able to:

  • Recover your email address or password
  • Access pins you created
  • Restore your account

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

1. Account Information

  • Email address
  • User ID
  • Authentication data (password hash)
  • Session tokens

2. User Content

  • All pins you created (including coordinates, status, and metadata)
  • Pin modification history
  • Sync queue entries

What May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or operational reasons:

  • Anonymous usage statistics: Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics data
  • Legal compliance: Records required by law (fraud prevention, abuse reports)
  • Backup archives: May take up to 90 days to fully purge from backup systems

How to Request Deletion

Option 1: Delete Individual Pins (Recommended)

If you only want to remove specific pins:

  1. Open the CCW Map app
  2. Tap on the pin you want to delete
  3. Select "Delete Pin" in the pin dialog

Note: This does NOT delete your account, only the selected pin.

Option 2: Delete Entire Account

To permanently delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Send an email to camilo@kyberneticlabs.com
  2. Use the subject line: "CCW Map Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information:
    • The email address associated with your CCW Map account
    • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

Email for Deletion Requests:

camilo@kyberneticlabs.com

Verification Process

To protect your account security, we will verify your identity:

  1. Email Verification: You must send the deletion request from the email address associated with your account
  2. Confirmation: We may ask you to confirm your request via a follow-up email
  3. Processing Time: Your data will be deleted within 30 days of receiving your verified request

After Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • You will be logged out of all devices
  • You cannot sign in with the same email address for 30 days
  • Pins you created will be removed from the map
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Alternative Options

If you're not sure about deleting your account, consider these alternatives:

  • Delete specific pins: Remove only the pins you want to delete (see Option 1 above)
  • Update your pins: Edit existing pins to correct information instead of deleting
  • Take a break: Simply stop using the app - your account remains inactive but can be reactivated anytime
